Interactive Experiments 🧪

Explore these experiments by topic. Expand steps and mark them completed to earn rewards!

Biology: Photosynthesis in Leaves 🌱

Observe starch formation in sunlight-exposed leaves.

  1. Boil a leaf, remove chlorophyll with alcohol.
  2. Rinse and add iodine.
  3. Blue-black color shows starch.

Biology: Osmosis with Potato 🥔

Demonstrate osmosis using salt water and potato slices.

  1. Cut potato slices.
  2. Place one in salt water, one in fresh water.
  3. Observe shrinkage vs swelling.

Biology: Germination 🌱

Observe seed sprouting under different conditions.

  1. Place seeds in moist cotton.
  2. Keep one in sunlight, one in dark.
  3. Compare growth.

Physics: Balloon Rocket 🚀

Newton’s Third Law demonstration.

  1. Thread straw on string.
  2. Tape balloon to straw.
  3. Release balloon.

Physics: Pendulum Motion ⏳

Observe periodic motion.

  1. Hang a weight on string.
  2. Pull and release.
  3. Observe oscillations.

Physics: Static Electricity ⚡

Use a balloon to attract paper bits.

  1. Rub balloon on hair.
  2. Bring near paper bits.
  3. Observe attraction.

Chemistry: Vinegar & Baking Soda Volcano 🌋

Acid-base reaction producing CO₂.

  1. Put baking soda in container.
  2. Add vinegar.
  3. Observe fizzing.

Chemistry: Rust Formation 🔩

Observe oxidation of iron.

  1. Place iron nail in water.
  2. Leave for days.
  3. Observe rust.

Chemistry: Litmus Test 🎨

Test acids and bases using litmus paper.

  1. Dip litmus paper in vinegar (acid).
  2. Observe red color.
  3. Dip litmus paper in baking soda solution (base).
  4. Observe blue color.

Chemistry: Crystal Formation 💎

Grow salt crystals from a solution.

  1. Dissolve salt in hot water.
  2. Pour into a jar and suspend a string.
  3. Leave for days.
  4. Observe crystals forming on string.

Chemistry: Density Layers 🥤

Create colorful density layers with liquids.

  1. Pour honey, dish soap, water, oil, and alcohol into a glass.
  2. Observe layers forming due to density differences.

Earth Science: Water Filtration 🌍

Learn how soil and sand filter dirty water.

  1. Layer gravel, sand, and charcoal in a bottle.
  2. Pour dirty water through.
  3. Observe cleaner water dripping out.

Earth Science: Mini Tornado 🌪️

Create a vortex in a bottle.

  1. Fill a bottle with water.
  2. Swirl it in circles.
  3. Observe tornado-like vortex.

Earth Science: Rock Cycle Simulation 🪨

Model the rock cycle with crayons.

  1. Shave crayons (sediments).
  2. Press together (sedimentary rock).
  3. Heat gently (metamorphic rock).
  4. Melt completely (igneous rock).

Earth Science: Solar Oven ☀️

Use sunlight to cook food.

  1. Line a box with foil.
  2. Place food inside.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap.
  4. Leave in sunlight to heat.

Earth Science: Cloud in a Bottle ☁️

Create condensation clouds.

  1. Put warm water in a bottle.
  2. Add a lit match briefly, then cap.
  3. Squeeze and release bottle.
  4. Observe cloud formation.
